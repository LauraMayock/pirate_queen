Irelands Pirate Queen

Who was Grace O'Malley?

  • 1530
    Born
    In the lorship of Umhall, Co Mayo, daughter of the chieftain Owen (Dudara) O’Malley from whom she learned her seafaring expertise.
  • 1546
    Married
    Given in a politically motivated marriage to Donal of the Battles O’Flaherty, tanaiste of the Clan O’Flaherty in his castle of Bunowen, in Connemara.
  • 1547-1552
    Mother of two
    Gives birth to 2 sons, Owen and Murrough and a daughter, Margaret.
  • 1560
    Title 4
    Donal killed in an inter-clan dispute. Grace avenges his death and assumes leadership of his clan on behalf of her sons.
